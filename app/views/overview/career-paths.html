Product & delivery career paths

NHS Digital follows industry standards within product & delivery roles and is aligned with similar roles across government.

What are the roles and bands?

There are 5 roles across product & delivery management:

  • Band 7 - Associate Product Manager
  • Band 8A – Product Manager
  • Band 8B – Senior Product Manager
  • Band 8C – Lead Product Manager
  • Band 8D - Head of Product

There are 5 roles across delivery management:

  • Band 7 - Associate Delivery Manager
  • Band 8A – Delivery Manager
  • Band 8B – Senior Delivery Manager
  • Band 8C – Lead Delivery Manager
  • Band 8D - Head of Delivery

Read more about salaries on NHS Employers.

Where can I find skills and competencies?

The Digital, Data and Technology (DDaT) framework publishes all the roles within the profession and the skills needed to do them. You can find out more about what product managers do and the skills they need.
